Market Insights

Price Trends Analysis

The average price per square foot has shown a steady increase over the past year, reaching $498/sqft in the most recent month. This represents a 4.3% increase compared to 12 months ago, indicating a healthy appreciation rate in property values.

Inventory & Sales Analysis

The current inventory level of 483 active listings represents a slight increase from the previous month. With an average of 128 sales per month, the current absorption rate is approximately 3.5 months, which indicates a balanced market with a slight advantage for sellers.

Market Efficiency

Properties are selling in an average of 29 days, which is 8.2% faster than the same period last year. This indicates increasing market efficiency and buyer demand, particularly in the North End and Westview neighborhoods which show the strongest price appreciation.
